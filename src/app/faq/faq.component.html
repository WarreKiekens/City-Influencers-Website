Frequently Asked Questions

The most common questions about the City Influencer platform and what we can do for you.

What is the City Influencer platform? City Influencers is a platform where one can immediately promote their city with local influencers. They are rewarded for this and local business gets a boost.
How can I, as a city, sign up to participate? As a city you can apply through the sign up page, after this the application will be forwarded to us. You will be notified via email when you can log in to the system.
How can I, as a person, sign up to participate? Potential new influencers can download the app here. In the app, they can then register.
How are influencers rewarded? There is a point system for the influencers, they earn points by the posts they make for a city/ for cities. They can then later convert these points into vouchers to spend at local merchants.
How can I, as a city, request a demo? You can make a request through the contact form, by mail (info@fairville.be) or by phone (09 362 71 25).
How can I communicate with you? You can contact us through the contact form, by mail (info@fairville.be) or by phone (09 362 71 25).
